University Graduates and their Pay over the Years

The culture around university education has long been entrenched in the idea of prestige and pragatism. In Singapore, a large concern around the university one attends and even the degree one chooses to study is based on how much it pays in the future (citation).

Prestige attached to the name of a university could also affect the ammount of pay a university graduate receives, usually based on whether it is a private or state university.

The degree one studies also affects the pay a graduate will receive. This is a result of industry needs and trends.
